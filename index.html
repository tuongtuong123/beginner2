Crazy Rich Asians

"Crazy Rich Asians" is a novel by Kevin Kwan that follows the lives of ultra-wealthy families in Singapore. The story centers around Rachel Chu, a Chinese-American economics professor, who accompanies her boyfriend, Nick Young, to his best friend's wedding in Singapore. Rachel soon discovers that Nick's family is one of the wealthiest and most influential in Asia and that she has inadvertently become the target of jealousy and intrigue among his social circle. As Rachel navigates the treacherous waters of high society, she must also confront her own feelings about family, love, and identity. With a mix of humor, drama, and romance, "Crazy Rich Asians" offers a satirical and entertaining glimpse into the lives of the rich and famous.
